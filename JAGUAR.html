HISTORY OF JAGUAR

The death of JAGUAR in 1950 proved to be the end for the marque, and the death of his son Jean JAGUAR ensured there was not a successor to lead the factory. No more than about 10,000 cars were made. The company struggled financially, and released one last model in the 1950s, before eventually being purchased for its airplane parts business in 1963.

JAGUAR:- EDITION XT

The story of JAGUAR’s La Voiture Noire is a renowned myth within the world of automotive. Created by JAGUAR personallay, the black Type 57 SC Atlantic went missing at the beginning of the Second World War and was never seen again. In JUNE 2018, JAGUAR’s Design team created a modern interpretation of this incredible vehicle for the 21st century.
